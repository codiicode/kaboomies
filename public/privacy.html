Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. In this policy, "we" / "us" refers to the KABOOMIES project.

This policy explains what data KABOOMIES (kaboomies.fun) collects and how we use it. You can play as a guest with minimal data, or sign in for a persistent profile.

1. What we collect

DataWhenWhy
Display name you chooseAlwaysShow you in the arena and on leaderboards
Gameplay data (free-chip balance, XP, level, wins/kills, quests, streaks)When signed inSave your progress and rankings
Solana wallet addressIf you connect a walletIdentify your account and verify a signed login
Social profile (e.g. X/Twitter username & avatar, or email)If you log in via PrivyShow who you're signed in as
Basic technical data (IP, browser, approximate connection info)AlwaysRun the service, security, abuse prevention

2. Voice chat

In-game voice chat is peer-to-peer (WebRTC) and push-to-talk / opt-in. We do not record, store, or listen to your audio; it is transmitted directly between players' browsers.

3. Local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember preferences and your session (e.g. chosen character, cached login). This stays on your device.

4. Third parties

We rely on these providers, who process data on our behalf or as part of their service:

  • Privy — authentication (email / social / wallet login) and any embedded wallet.
  • Railway — hosting and infrastructure.
  • Supabase — optional database mirror for balances/stats (if enabled).
  • Solana network — if you connect a wallet, your public address and on-chain activity are inherently public on the blockchain.
  • X/Twitter, Google, Discord, etc. — if you choose to log in with them via Privy, per their own policies.

5. How we use data

To operate the Game, save progress, show leaderboards and identity, prevent cheating and abuse, and improve the experience. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Retention & your choices

Signed-in profile data is kept while your account is active. Play as a guest to minimize the data tied to you. You can disconnect or log out at any time, which stops new profile data from being associated with your session.

7. Children

The Game is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
